About Our Restaurant

The HistoryOf Our Restaurant

Los Pollos Hermanos a fast-food restaurant chain that specialized in fried chicken, operating across the southwestern United States. The name, pronounced is ungrammatical Spanish for "The Chicken Brothers." Founded by Gustavo Fring and Max Arciniega , the restaurant chain had fourteen locations throughout the southwest and Gustavo Fring's major business concern. Los Pollos Hermanos was a subsidiary of Madrigal Electromotive, a German conglomerate with an ownership stake in the company.
